Historical Sites in Kerala

Historical Monuments

1. Padmanabhapuram Palace

Details: A stunning wooden palace built in the 16th century, known for its intricate architecture and historical significance.

2. Fort Kochi

Details: Known for its colonial architecture and historical landmarks, including St. Francis Church and the Chinese fishing nets.

Cultural Heritage

1. Hill Palace

Details: The former residence of the Kochi royal family, now a museum showcasing royal artifacts and the lush surrounding gardens.

2. Kuthiramalika Palace

Details: A beautiful palace built by Maharaja Swathi Thirunal, known for its unique architecture and rich history.

Markets and Shopping

1. Jew Town

Details: Famous for its antique shops and spice markets, offering a glimpse into the rich trading history of Kerala.

2. Lulu Mall

Details: One of the largest shopping malls in India, offering a mix of international brands, dining options, and entertainment.
